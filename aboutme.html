About Me

Being able to fly a plane is one of my talents. I am currently learning how to fly a plane with the Coachella Valley Youth Aviation Education Program which is helping me get my private pilot’s license. After I get my license and graduate high school I plan on going to College of the Desert for two years and live with my friends. After college, I will go on my mission for the LDS church which will last another two years hopefully in another country. Finally, after all of that is done I hope to join the Coast Guard to fly helicopters for them.


Some of my major accomplishments are being accepted into the Aviation Education Program and becoming an Eagle Scout. I became a boy scout at 11 and worked hard to get all my requirements completed. I moved about halfway through my progress and then when I became a Life Scout I had to wait six months which was a problem because the LDS church would cut off ties with the Boy Scouts of America in 4 months. I had to join a new troop in order to become an Eagle Scout. It was all difficult but it helped me with lots. Some of my characteristics that help with being a pilot are that I am observant, I can think quickly, I have good team-working skills, and attention to detail. However, I think some words that would describe me from the Character Trait list are: calm, alert, creative, patient, trustworthy, loyal, helpful, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean, and reverent. The majority of what I listed was actually just the Scout Law which all Boy/Eagle Scouts have to memorize.


I hopefully will become a helicopter pilot like my dad. I really enjoy flying airplanes and knowing how they work. Thank you for viewing this about me page and my website.
